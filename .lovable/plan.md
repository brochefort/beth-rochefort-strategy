

# Beth Rochefort — Personal Website

## Overview
A clean, professional single-page personal website showcasing Beth's expertise as a learning technology strategist and digital transformation leader. Blues and greens color palette, mobile-first, editorial quality design.

## Design System
- **Colors**: Deep blue (#1e3a5f), sage green (#7c9a82), soft teal (#5b9ea6), off-white background (#f8f9fa), near-black text (#1a1a2e)
- **Typography**: Inter font family, generous whitespace
- **Style**: Professional but human, clean cards with subtle shadows, smooth scroll navigation

## Sections (Single Page, Sticky Nav)

1. **Navigation** — Sticky header with logo image, section links, LinkedIn button. Mobile hamburger menu.

2. **Hero** — Name, title, location, one-liner quote, tagline pills/badges, LinkedIn CTA button.

3. **What I Do** — 3-column card grid (Learning Design, Digital Transformation, Strategy & Ecosystem Building).

4. **My Perspective** — Full-width dark blue/teal background with pull quote and attribution.

5. **What I'm Working On Now** — 5 cards in a responsive grid with titles and descriptions.

6. **Career Highlights** — Grid of highlight items with accent checkmark icons (12 items).

7. **What Didn't Work** — Single card with accent left border, 4 honest lessons learned.

8. **Portfolio** — 3 placeholder cards with "Coming Soon" note.

9. **Contact** — Contact form (Name, Email, Message, Send button with validation), LinkedIn link, email display.

## Key Details
- Smooth scroll navigation between sections
- Contact form with client-side validation (Zod) — form submissions stored locally or shown as toast confirmation (no backend initially)
- Fully responsive: mobile-first with breakpoints for tablet and desktop
- Subtle animations on scroll (fade-in for sections)
- All content provided above will be hardcoded into components
